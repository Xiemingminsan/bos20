Film Productions

We love having the opportunity to create publicity spots with agencies and clients. We offer support towards finding the best communicative solutions, beginning with an idea and later producing it into a memorable audiovisual piece that will generate an impact onto audiences.

PRE-VIS

Our exclusive directors are specialized in various areas such as; beauty, food, restaurants, auto industry and fashion. Our directors create a pre-visualization deck of a solicited project during its initial quoting phase.

PRE-PRO AND PRODUCTION

There’s not a single project that is too complex or impossible in times for us to handle. We have a solid production infrastructure that is conformed of over 30 full-time employees. We also own a full equipment rental facility ‘Acolite’, which gives us dominance during filming and shooting.

POST-PRODUCTION

Our post-production department is essential when it comes to finalizing a project. Our team is staffed with eight post-production editors who carry out various specialties. Our post house manages the finer details for publicity pieces.

CONTENT [&] FILM

Our creative content department makes it a standing priority to develop and produce feature films along with other content consuming formats. Several of our projects have been developed in partnership with seasoned Hollywood studio industry writers.

BRANDED CONTENT

If you are a brand and are trying to connect your consumers to your product in a different way, under new and engaging formats - we are here to help! We can produce and show you how your brand can connect to real stories that inspire and transcend onto consumers and new followers.

CONTENT DISTRIBUTION

We start connecting with distributors from the treatment phase of a script. We have distributors who represent our work in the US who are responsible for selling and placing our projects at international movie theaters or on digital platforms such as iTunes, Vimeo On-Demand and Amazon Prime. Because of these representations, we are able to reach millions of people worldwide with our in-house content.

DIGITAL CONTENT

Consumers expect to watch new and engaging audiovisual content through their social and digital media platforms. We want to be your partner and help you create unique content, under the frequency that your markets demand it. Let’s create your unique pieces, under the highest quality production, together!

TYPES OF VIDEOS

Explanatory videos, testimonies, commercials, user content and promotional videos.

HIGH QUALITY

Short and feature length audiovisual formats, with a high level of cinematic production quality.
